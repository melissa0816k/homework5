Hongkongers enjoy world's longest life expectancy

Presented by Melissa KO

Japanese womenhad the world’s longest average life expectancy since 1985; however; this changed in 2011.The average lifespan for women in Hong Kong is 87.32 years, and local men on average can expect to live to 81.24. Japanese women took second place at 87.05, while Icelandic and Swiss men shared the second position in the men’s category at 81 years.

Source from SCMP.
